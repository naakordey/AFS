Who We Are

The Africa Fintech Studio is positioned as a Pan-African fintech studio with a mission to scout and scale highly promising fintech startups through multiple growth pipelines.

The Africa Fintech Studio also forms part of the Africa Ventures and Fintech Studio’s initiative to scale highly promising and sustainably viable ventures across Africa.

Our Goal

We are positioned to become a pan-African Fintech powerhouse for highly scalable fintech ventures.

Our Mission

We are here to scout, support and scale founders, and products providing innovative financial services across Africa through multiple growth pipelines.

What we do

Why AFS

At the Africa Fintech Studio, we are focused on scouting, funneling and onboarding the right teams delivering great products to scale through different support and growth pipelines such as access to the right capital to scale, business and growth support, access to market, regulatory and compliance and technical support.

What Makes us Outstanding

Over the past year, our unique mechanism of empowering and investing in early-stage FinTech startups to access talent, capital and demand, as well as effective progressive policies and regulations designed to enable FinTech startup growth in the Pan-Africa market.

Our Fintech Studio

The Fintech Studio is established purposely to scout and scale high-growth potential fintech startups that have the ability to scale largely across the continent. The fintech Studio acts as a core part of the entire Africa Fintech Studio with other initiatives acting as vehicles to driving and meeting its overall goals and objectives. The Fintech Studio act and operate as a venture-backed accelerator provided the necessary ecosystem for startups in our portfolio to scale through multiple arrangements such as;

  • Access to market through FIRA and other AFS partners.
  • Investor readiness and funding through investment partners.
  • Access to physical and technical infrastructure to build and scale startups.
  • Access to business development advisory and support.
  • Regulatory and compliance advisory.

FIRA(Financial Inclusion for Rural Africa)

Financial Inclusion for Rural Africa: Our goal is to ensure financial inclusion for underserved communities in rural Africa through partnered programs and initiatives. Our flagship FIRA is designed to help us reach out to rural communities in Africa, educating them on financial products and services, technologies that will enhance their livelihoods.

Funding

We invest in products and teams providing solutions impact and for-profit innovations that are driving financial inclusion and innovation in Africa.
